## 3. Technical SEO Checklist (Immediate Actions)

*   [x] **Metadata**: Update `layout.js` with distinct, keyword-rich titles/descriptions.
*   [x] **Schema Markup**: Implement `Organization`, `LocalBusiness`, and `Service` schema (Done in `layout.js`, refine `Service`).
*   [ ] **Sitemap**: Ensure `sitemap.xml` is auto-generated and accurate (Current `sitemap.js` is good).
*   [ ] **Robots.txt**: Optimize to allow crawling of main content but block admin/api.
*   [ ] **Core Web Vitals**: Minimize JS execution (use `next/dynamic` for heavy components), optimize images (`next/image` with `sizes` prop).
*   [ ] **Canonical Tags**: Ensure self-referencing canonicals on all pages to prevent duplicate content issues.

## 4. Off-Page & Backlink Strategy

*   **Guest Blogging**: Pitch to men's fashion blogs (e.g., Dappered, FashionBeans) about "The resurgence of bespoke tailoring in leather".
*   **Press Releases**: Announce "ATSAS Launches Sustainable Leather Initiative".
*   **Local Citations**: Register on Yell, Yelp, Google My Business, Scoot (UK specific).
*   **Social Signals**: Create Pinterest boards for "Leather Jacket Inspo 2026" linking back to product pages.

## 5. Local SEO (London/UK)

*   **Google My Business**: Optimize profile with photos of the workshop, team, and raw materials.
*   **Local Keywords**: Use "Leather Jacket Repair London", "Bespoke Tailor Shoreditch", "UK Leather Factory" in footer and contact page.
*   **Reviews**: detailed review generation strategy (email follow-ups).

## 6. International SEO

*   **Hreflang**: If expanding to EU/US, implement `hreflang` tags (e.g., `en-us`, `en-gb`).
*   **Currency/Shipping**: Ensure clear info on "Worldwide Shipping" and "USD/EUR" pricing visibility.

## 7. Performance Tracking

*   **Google Search Console**: Monitor "Performance" tab for impressions/clicks.
*   **Google Analytics 4**: Set up "Purchase" and "Lead Form Submit" conversions.
*   **Rank Tracking**: Use Ahrefs/SEMrush to track keyword movements weekly.
